Document Generator for Salesforce Template

Welcome to Our Document Generator Service

Our Document Generator API is designed to help you convert Salesforce templates into PDF files with ease. This service is perfect for integrating with your Salesforce instance and automating document generation.

Features

  • Easy integration with Salesforce templates
  • High-quality PDF conversion
  • Customizable output to fit your needs
  • Supports various template formats

How It Works

To use our API, follow these simple steps:

  1. Set up your Salesforce template.
  2. Integrate our API into your application.
  3. Send the template data to our API.
  4. Receive and download the generated PDF file.
